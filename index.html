As a classically trained, dually credentialed teacher, I help guide both new and experienced homeschooling families in designing a homeschool program that embodies their personal educational philosophy. Together we will create the groundwork for your homeschool program that is rooted in creativity, commitment and change for all learning. Along with professional tutoring and group class sessions, we also explore curriculum best suited for your child, learning supports and accommodations needed, as well as a daily, weekly and monthly rhythm that provides a sense of exploration and cadence for success throughout the day.
