-Inspirational quote here maybe?- About me:

I have my K-8 General Education Teaching Credential as well as my 3-22 yr. old Mild/Moderate Special Education Teaching Credential. I also have my RICA certification and am highly trained in the DIBELS assessments. I taught for 7 years prior to homeschooling my own five children for the past 9 years. I have taught part time for the past two years at a local homeschooling charter school collaborating with students and families on their homeschooling journey. I recognize that there are numerous reasons we choose to homeschool our children, and I want to help you navigate that process in a way that creates both a positive learning environment at home for you and your student, as well as success for their futures.
